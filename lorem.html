📜 What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is the standard for dummy text (placeholder text) widely used in design and publishing. Starting with "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit...", it originated from a Latin document that was the standard dummy text for the printing industry since the mid-1500s.

This text became the industry standard because it allows focusing on visual effects rather than content meaning when evaluating designs and layouts. Designers and developers can objectively judge fonts, spacing, and layout without being distracted by content significance.

🎨 Why Dummy Text is Needed in Design

When designing websites or apps, you often need to design layout and structure before actual content is ready. Using dummy text like Lorem Ipsum lets you place approximate content in spaces where real data will go, enabling visual evaluation.

Especially in responsive web design, testing layouts at various screen sizes or adjusting font sizes and line heights requires dummy text with similar characteristics to real content. This allows previewing final product readability and aesthetic balance.

⚠️ Lorem Ipsum Usage Precautions

While Lorem Ipsum is standard in publishing and design, using it in actual services or marketing materials is inappropriate. Meaningless text in real environments confuses users and harms service quality.
